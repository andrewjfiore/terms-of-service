# Privacy Policy

## Personal AI Assistant Communication Service

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Andrew Fiore ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and protects information when my personal AI assistant contacts you via SMS, MMS, or voice call using the phone number associated with this service. This assistant is powered by OpenClaw and uses Twilio as the telecommunications provider.

By continuing to engage with messages or calls from this service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy. You may opt out at any time as described below.

## 2. Information I Collect

When my AI assistant communicates with you, the following information may be collected:

### Phone Number

- Your phone number is used to send and receive messages or place and receive calls.

### Message Content

- The content of SMS and MMS messages exchanged between you and the assistant is processed to generate relevant responses.

### Call Data

- For voice calls, call duration, timestamps, and transcriptions of the conversation may be recorded for quality and context purposes.

### Interaction Metadata

- Twilio, as the telecommunications provider, may collect metadata such as timestamps, delivery status, and carrier information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

## 3. How I Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

- **Facilitating communication** — To send messages and place calls on my behalf for scheduling, reminders, follow-ups, appointment booking, and general inquiries.
- **Providing context** — To maintain conversational context so the assistant can respond appropriately to ongoing exchanges.
- **Personal recordkeeping** — To keep a log of communications for my own reference.

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. I do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond the direct communication described above.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This service relies on the following third-party providers to function. Each has its own privacy practices:

### Twilio

- Provides the telecommunications infrastructure for SMS and voice calls.
- Processes your phone number and message or call data to deliver communications.
- Privacy policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy

### AI Model Providers

- Message content and call transcriptions may be sent to AI model providers such as Anthropic or OpenAI to generate responses.
- These providers process data according to their respective privacy policies and data retention terms.

### Text-to-Speech Providers

- For voice calls, a text-to-speech service such as ElevenLabs or OpenAI may be used to generate spoken responses.

## 5. Data Retention

- Message logs and call records are retained on my self-hosted server for as long as they are useful for maintaining conversational context and personal records.
- Twilio retains its own logs according to its data retention policies.
- You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me directly.

## 6. Data Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

- Securing my server infrastructure
- Using authenticated API connections with Twilio
- Restricting access to communication logs

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights and Opt-Out

You have the right to opt out of communications from this service at any time:

### SMS Opt-Out

- Reply STOP to any text message to immediately cease all future SMS messages.
- This is handled automatically by Twilio at the platform level.

### Voice Call Opt-Out

- During any call, you may verbally request not to be contacted again.
- You can also send a text to the number saying STOP.

### Data Deletion

- You may request that I delete any data associated with your phone number by contacting me directly.
- I will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

### Resume Communications

- If you previously opted out via STOP, you may resume communications by texting START to the same number.

## 8. Children's Privacy

- I do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

## 10. Contact Information
